TelcoNG overview

With consumers constantly on the lookout for the next best offer, service providers are challenged to stay profitable while meeting customer demand. To do this, service providers need a next-generation BSS/OSS platform that enhances their ability to maintain customer satisfaction, drive new revenue, and increase operational efficiency. To satisfy today’s customers, it is critical for service providers to offer a consistent and unified multi-channel experience by providing advanced capabilities and enhanced self service, as well as access to real-time information regarding usage, services, and charges. Market saturation and increased competition have also made it imperative for service providers to explore new revenue opportunities, including the launch of innovative consumer services, expansion into the B2B and Network sharing space, and the introduction of quad-play bundles. Finally, outsid forces have also made it critical for service providers to look inward at the cost of doing business and the optimization of their infrastructure and operations. By consolidating their applications onto a single platform, service providers can reduce integration and support costs.

Key benefits include:

Single and unified platform, modular and pre-integrated Unique functional BSS/OSS footprint including real-time Rating & Charging, Sales & Partner Management, Work Force & Process Flows Automation Using clouWay's experience in the CRM, billing, and self-service domains, TelcoNG BSS includes core functionality that covers the areas of Customer Information Management, Customer Self-Service, Customer Order Management, Customer Billing Management. TelcoNG BSS is an integral part of the TelcoNG end-to-end B/OSS offering and includes pre-integration with TelcoNG OSS, TelcoNG WSS, TelcoNG BI. This significantly reduces integration costs during implementation, brings necessary consistency of data and functions, and enables other beneficial opportunities. TelcoNG CRM helps Communications Service Providers improve service desk operations by automating and facilitating customer support, reducing time-to-resolution, optimizing front office operational expenses, and delivering a superior customer experience. TelcoNG CRM provides a comprehensive set of capabilities for end-to-end customer problem resolution. It streamlines the initial problem capture process by giving customer service representatives (CSRs) a complete picture of the customer’s account and interaction history, and thus fosters first-call problem resolution. It also provides visibility into the customer ticket lifecycle and enables the collection and analysis of customer feedback. TelcoNG WSS Workforce Management enables Communications Service Providers (CSPs) to manage field operations as part of the end-to-end service delivery lifecycle. This elevates the visibility of resource capacity and allows for business process orchestration and performance optimization, ultimately guaranteeing improved operational efficiency and reduced TCO. Workforce Management uses TelcoNG BI, TelcoNG BSS, TelcoNG OSS capabilities for business performance measurement and resource demand trending. This gives CSPs end-to-end visibility into business processes, real-time views, and statistical reports, and also enables them to execute KPI information aggregation and analysis and jeopardy management. Communications Service Providers (CSPs) worldwide are striving to deliver the latest converged services including IPTV, Multi-Play, and 4G. These content-rich offerings create significant revenue opportunities and differentiation. To this end, CSPs have invested heavily in the network and customer layers. They have capped legacy networks; built high-capacity, next-generation networks; and deployed next-generation, customer-facing systems. TelcoNG OSS has helped service providers bring systematic changes to their back-office systems, enabling them to generate new revenues, contain costs, and maintain customer centricity all at once.
